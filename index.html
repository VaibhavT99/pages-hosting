Here Are Some Facts About Cats
Scroll Down For More Fun Facts About Cats

Cats can jump up to 6 times their height

That’s right! Cats are really good jumpers and can jump really high. You can often see them on the top of a garden fence or on a really high wall!

They have a total of 18 toes

That’s a lot of toes! Cats have 5 toes on each of their front paws. They then have four toes on their back paws.

A cat can run up to 30mph

Cats are very speedy. They can run up to 30mph. That’s about as fast as a car will travel on a typical road!
